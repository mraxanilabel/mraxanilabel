Demo Submission Policy

Thank you for your interest in submitting your music to Mraxani Label!

We are a young and passionate independent record label, always on the lookout for new sounds and fresh talent. We welcome demo submissions from artists and producers of all levels and are committed to listening to all material we receive.

What We Are Looking For

We are open to demos in all genres that fit the artistic vision of our label. We encourage creativity and originality — even if your style doesn’t perfectly match our current roster.

How to Submit

Please send your demo by email to: mraxanilabel@gmail.com

In your email, include:

  • A private streaming link (SoundCloud, Dropbox, Google Drive, etc.) or downloadable link to your tracks
  • A short artist bio or press kit
  • Links to your social media and music platforms (if available)

Please do NOT send:

  • Large file attachments directly to email — use shared links instead
  • Public links with unlimited access (we prefer private/shared links)
